Our History


Ariel Arellanes was born and raised in Vista California. As a certified Estetician and Cosmetologist, she founded Saint Skin Salon & Spa, with the goal in mind to share her passion for skincare and wellness with others.

Ariel is a certified PCA and Dermalogica professional, and is a strong supporter of their product line and vision. She routinely attends national beauty expositions in San Diego to stay up to date on the latest skin care trends, techniques and products available on the market.
